About Our Valuation Practice

Independent, state-certified commercial real estate appraisal and consulting services.

Devin Shobe, MAI, ARA

Founder & Certified General Appraiser

Devin Shobe founded the valuation practice to deliver rigorous, data-driven real estate appraisal solutions. With extensive background analyzing commercial assets, agricultural operations, and specialized infrastructure, the practice is built on technical accuracy and independent market tracking.

Prior to establishing the independent fee practice, extensive experience was gained managing complex institutional valuations, regulatory lending reviews, and collateral risk metrics. This background ensures that every report meets the highest institutional scrutiny required by regional lenders, AMCs, and regulatory agencies.

The practice specializes in comprehensive narrative valuations across diverse property types including community retail infrastructure, multi-family complexes, industrial manufacturing facilities, complex agricultural assets, and development land. Valuations are engineered for a wide array of application scopes, including mortgage financing, estate planning, tax assessment, and litigation defense.

Verified Credentials & Licensing

Full compliance with federal and state regulatory infrastructure guarantees defensible valuations.

State Certified General

Active and fully credentialed Certified General Real Estate Appraiser in Ohio and Indiana. Authorized to value all types of commercial, industrial, and agricultural real property regardless of transaction value or complexity.

MAI Designation

Held by advanced commercial valuation professionals, the MAI designation from the Appraisal Institute signifies deep technical competence, rigorous peer review, and adherence to professional ethics standards well exceeding baseline licensing.

ARA Designation

The Accredited Rural Appraiser (ARA) designation from the ASFMRA denotes expert proficiency in complex agricultural operations, rural agribusiness, land resource economics, and specialized permanent planting valuations.
