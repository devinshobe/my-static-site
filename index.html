Devin Shobe is a highly qualified Collateral Analyst and Certified General Real Estate Appraiser specializing in complex agricultural and commercial real estate valuation for lending and litigation parameters. Backed by institutional standard-setting and industry leadership, the practice delivers rigorous, data-driven real property analytics built to survive intensive third-party review.

Currently serving as a Collateral Analyst at Farm Credit Mid-America, Devin excels in large-scale collateral appraisals, financial asset metrics, and process innovation. Notably, he has contributed directly as an active member of the department's Forms Team, engineering state-of-the-art appraisal templates and instructing valuation staff on implementation across regional divisions. His work also includes driving commercial workshops and refining advanced AgWare platform commercial infrastructure before structural market launch.

Devin's extensive experience spans complex ag-business properties, production facilities, commercial infrastructure, industrial real estate, and development tracts. Driven by a deep professional passion for training and educational development, he acts as an active supervisor for multiple appraisal trainees, standardizing high-level valuation excellence for the next generation of appraisers.

Demonstrating active industry commitment, Devin serves on the national ASFMRA Government Relations Committee and is the Immediate Past President of the Ohio Chapter of the ASFMRA, actively molding regional and national public policy frameworks affecting agricultural valuation infrastructure.
